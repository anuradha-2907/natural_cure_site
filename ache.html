HEADACHE REMEDIES

Remedies :-

1.TURN DOWN THE LIGHTS:-

Some people who get headaches become sensitive to light. Bright office lights or even the bright light from a smartphone may make symptoms worse. It may help to rest in a dark or dimly lit room while recovering from a headache.

2.COCONUT WATER:-

Coconut water are natural cures for dehydration and relieve headaches related to dehydration.

They are natural sources of electrolytes, without the added sugar or artificial sweeteners that are found in many commercial electrolyte beverages.
